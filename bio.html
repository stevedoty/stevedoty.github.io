BIO

I was born and raise in Redwood Shores, Ca. I spent much of my childhood around tech, be it games or computers. Being outside was the other half of the coin, playing sports, hiking, swimming, or just getting into trouble.

We can learn so much by being around various cultures and I believe it makes us stronger individuals and better team players.
